Professional Bodies

Membership Plans
& Professional Bodies

CIPS
Chartered Institute of Procurement & Supply
CIPS — UK-based global procurement body
The world's largest professional procurement body. Student membership provides access to knowledge resources, procurement frameworks, and global networking.
🎯 Join: Year 4/ Post Graduate
  • CIPS Level 4 Diploma in Procurement & Supply
  • Access to CIPS Knowledge Hub — free articles & guides
  • CIPS student member pricing on all qualifications
  • Global procurement community & forums
  • Ethical procurement framework resources
ASCM
Association for Supply Chain Management
ASCM — USA-based global SCM body (formerly APICS)
Home of the CSCP and SCOR framework. Student membership provides access to supply chain learning resources, SCOR guides, and industry connections.
🎯 Join: Year 4/Post Graduate
  • SCOR framework free guide download
  • CSCP — Certified Supply Chain Professional pathway
  • ASCM student discount on all certifications
  • Access to supply chain research library
  • Lean, Six Sigma & SCOR best practices
GIPS
Ghana Institute of Procurement & Supply
GIPS / SGIPS — Ghana's national procurement body
Ghana's own procurement professional body. Student membership (SGIPS) connects you to Ghana's procurement regulatory updates, industry forums, and local hiring networks.
🎯 Join: Year 1-2-3 · Now
  • SGIPS student member — affordable fee
  • Ghana procurement regulatory updates
  • Industry forums & policy workshops in Kumasi
  • Mentorship from senior procurement professionals
  • Direct connection to Ghana's top employers
